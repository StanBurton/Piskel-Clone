New Sprite *

Piskel is a free online editor for animated sprites & pixel art

Create animations in your browser. Try an example, use Google sign in to access your gallery or simply create a new sprite.

Example sprites

Here are some examples of user submitted sprites. Click on a preview to open the sprite in the editor.

Brush sizes

You have 4 types of brush thickness

Mousewheel on canvas

When drawing, you can zoom in and zoom out the canvas in a way most convenient for you.

Export .gif

You can export your current session to gif. format.

Fullscreen mode

Turns on in the animation playback window.

Save current session

If you restart your browser or application, your session will be restored.

Frame management

Available copying, deleting and rearranging frames

Canvas hover

You will always see which pixel the mouse is on.

Resize

You can increase your working field.
